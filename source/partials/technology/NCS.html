NCS

Noise correction for sCMOS (NCS) is a denoising algorithm designed specifically for the scientific Complementary Metal–Oxide–Semiconductor (sCMOS) camera. sCMOS have pixel-dependent noise. NCS considers the statistics of individual pixels and denoise the image based on the frequency constraint on the optical system. As a result, NCS maximally preserves the fidelity of the image without introducing any artifacts.
